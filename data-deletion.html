Data Deletion

This page explains how an authorized user can revoke TikTok access and request deletion of information stored by Island Besties Publisher.

1. Revoke TikTok access

The authorized account owner may revoke Island Besties Publisher’s access through TikTok account settings. After access is revoked, the application can no longer perform TikTok actions unless authorization is granted again.

Revoking access does not automatically remove information already stored by Island Besties Publisher and does not delete videos or drafts hosted directly by TikTok.

2. Request deletion

Send an email to islandbestiescy@gmail.com with the subject:

Island Besties Publisher Data Deletion Request

Please include the TikTok username or account identifier used to authorize the application and a brief description of the information you want deleted. Do not send passwords, OAuth tokens, access tokens, or client secrets by email.

3. Information that may be deleted

  • stored TikTok OAuth access and refresh credentials;
  • the authorized TikTok account identifier and basic profile metadata;
  • Telegram message identifiers and caption metadata;
  • upload records, TikTok publication identifiers, and status information;
  • technical error records associated with the authorization;
  • other configuration information associated with the connected account.

Temporary video files are removed from the temporary processing environment after the publishing workflow completes.

4. Processing time

Verified deletion requests are normally completed within 30 days. Limited records may be retained when reasonably necessary for security, fraud prevention, legal compliance, or investigation of misuse.

5. TikTok-hosted content

Deleting information stored by Island Besties Publisher does not delete videos, drafts, profile information, or other data stored directly by TikTok. Content hosted by TikTok must be managed through TikTok’s official applications or account controls.

6. Effect of deletion

After OAuth credentials are deleted, automated TikTok draft uploads stop for the affected account. To use the feature again, the account owner must complete TikTok authorization again. Deleting publishing records does not reverse completed publications on TikTok or other connected platforms.
