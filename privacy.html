Privacy Policy

This policy explains how Island Besties Publisher processes information when authorized project members connect TikTok and use the private publishing workflow.

1. Application scope

Island Besties Publisher is a private social media publishing tool operated for the Island Besties creator project. It does not offer public registration and is not made available to unrelated third parties.

2. TikTok services used

The application may use TikTok Login Kit and the TikTok Content Posting API to authorize the official Island Besties TikTok account and upload approved short-form videos as drafts for final review and posting in the TikTok app.

3. Information processed

Depending on the permissions granted, the application may process:

  • TikTok authorization tokens and the account identifier associated with the authorized account;
  • basic account information made available by TikTok, such as display name and avatar;
  • videos, captions, file metadata, Telegram message identifiers, and temporary media references submitted by authorized project members;
  • upload status, TikTok publication identifiers, operational logs, and limited error details.

The application does not request or store TikTok passwords.

4. How information is used

Information is used only to verify the authorized TikTok account, upload content selected by authorized project members, report success or errors, maintain security, and support the publishing workflow. It is not used for advertising, profiling, surveillance, or resale.

5. Data sharing

The application does not sell, rent, or trade personal information. Information is shared only with services required to complete the requested workflow, including TikTok, Telegram, GitHub, Meta, Instagram, Facebook, Google, and YouTube, each under its own terms and privacy policy.

6. Storage and security

Authorization credentials are stored as protected secrets and are not intentionally written to application logs. Video files are processed temporarily for publication and removed from the temporary processing environment after the workflow completes. Limited operational records may be retained for troubleshooting, security, and duplicate prevention.

7. Retention

TikTok authorization credentials are retained only while the integration remains authorized and necessary. Operational metadata is periodically reviewed and removed when no longer needed. Verified deletion requests are normally completed within 30 days.

8. User control and revocation

The authorized account owner may revoke the application’s TikTok access through TikTok account settings. Revoking access prevents future TikTok actions but does not automatically delete content already hosted by TikTok or information stored by Island Besties Publisher.

9. Data deletion

Deletion instructions are available on the Data Deletion page. Requests may also be sent to islandbestiescy@gmail.com.

10. Children

The application is a private operational tool and is not directed to children.

11. Changes

This policy may be updated when the workflow, applicable law, or connected platform requirements change. The effective date will be updated accordingly.
