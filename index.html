Island Besties Publisher helps authorized members of the Island Besties creator project send approved short-form videos from a private Telegram workspace to the project’s official social media channels.

The application is not open to the public, does not provide public registration, and does not publish to unrelated third-party accounts.

Authorized content only

Only project members may submit videos they own or are permitted to publish.

TikTok workflow

Approved videos may be uploaded to the authorized TikTok account as drafts for final review and posting in the TikTok app.

Privacy first

Credentials are kept private, media is processed only for publication, and temporary files are removed after processing.

Contact

Questions about the application, privacy, authorization, or data deletion may be sent to islandbestiescy@gmail.com.
